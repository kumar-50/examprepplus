# RRB NTPC Syllabus 2025 (CBT 1 and CBT 2)

## Exam Overview

- **Exam Name**: RRB NTPC (Railway Recruitment Board - Non-Technical Popular Categories)
- **Total Vacancies**: 8,868 positions
- **Exam Mode**: Online Computer-Based Test (CBT)
- **Languages**: Available in 15 languages
- **Negative Marking**: 1/3 mark deducted per wrong answer

## Selection Process

1. CBT 1 (Computer Based Test - Stage 1)
2. CBT 2 (Computer Based Test - Stage 2)
3. CBAT/Typing Test (for specific posts)
4. Document Verification
5. Medical Examination

## Exam Pattern

### CBT 1 (Stage 1)
- **Total Questions**: 100
- **Total Marks**: 100
- **Duration**: 90 minutes
- **Subjects**: 3 (Mathematics, General Intelligence & Reasoning, General Awareness)
- **Negative Marking**: 1/3 mark deducted for each wrong answer

### CBT 2 (Stage 2)
- **Total Questions**: 120
- **Total Marks**: 120
- **Duration**: 90 minutes
- **Subjects**: 3 (Mathematics, General Intelligence & Reasoning, General Awareness)
- **Negative Marking**: 1/3 mark deducted for each wrong answer

**Note**: The syllabus is identical for both CBT 1 and CBT 2 stages, with only the question count varying.

## Preparation Tips

1. **Plan a study schedule**: Create a comprehensive study plan covering all topics with time allocation.

2. **Focus on high-weightage topics**: Prioritize Current Affairs and General Science in GA section.

3. **Practice regularly**: Solve previous year papers and mock tests to understand exam patterns.

4. **Current Affairs**: Stay updated with daily current affairs, especially railway-related news.

5. **General Science**: Focus on 10th standard CBSE level Physics, Chemistry, and Biology.

6. **Mathematics**: Master fundamentals and practice speed calculation techniques.

7. **Time management**: Practice completing 100 questions in 90 minutes for CBT 1.

8. **Negative marking awareness**: Avoid random guessing due to 1/3 negative marking.

9. **Revision**: Regular revision of formulas, shortcuts, and important facts.

10. **Stay healthy**: Maintain good health and stay hydrated during preparation.

## Important Notes

- Same syllabus for both Graduate and Undergraduate level posts
- Focus on accuracy over speed due to negative marking
- Computer basics is an important topic in General Awareness
- Railway-related current affairs carry special importance
- Practice with bilingual question papers if appearing in regional language
